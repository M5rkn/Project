Our History
Like any other unique place, we have our own special history. The idea of the restaurant came to the founders unexpectedly. While walking in the forest, the creator of our restaurant got stuck hundreds of kilometers away from the nearest settlement. Far from civilization and communication, they had to temporarily settle into a simple lifestyle, sourcing and preparing their own food.
